Building Bridges Between UX and AI

I'm a UX Architect with a developer background, currently deepening my expertise at the intersection of user experience and artificial intelligence. My work centers on ensuring AI serves people equitably through inclusive design practices and ethical considerations.

From developer to UX generalist to UX architect, my journey has taught me that the most powerful technology is built with human dignity at its core. Now I'm exploring how AI can enhance research, accelerate insights, and help us create better experiences—while never losing sight of the diverse communities we serve.
